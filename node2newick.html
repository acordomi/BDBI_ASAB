node_to_newick

Description

Print a tree in Newick format from a nodes dictionary. The writting can be recursive (this is the easiest solution). You should open a parenthesis the first time you visit a node, separate the left and the right subtrees with a comma and close the parenthesis when you are done displaying the right group. In recursive terms, it means you open a parenthesis when entering the node, the function then calls itself to explore the left side and emits a list when coming back from this exploration, you insert a coma, the function calls itself again to explore the right side to emit another list, and close the parentesis.

Tests

    >>> node2newick(1, newick_file2nodes("tree_abcdef.dnd"))
