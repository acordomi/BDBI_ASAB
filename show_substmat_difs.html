show_substmat_difs

Description

Create a function that returns a list of tuples with pairs of amino acids and the non-zero difference in the substitution scores. The output should be sorted by absolute difference. In case of positive and negative values for the same value, positive numbers go first and negative second (i.e. a possible order could be 4, -4, 3, -3, 2, -2 ...). See tests. Compare the BLOSUM62 and the BLOSUM45 matrices.

Hint

The data that you want to display is the following and in this order (though in a different format) 


    F F 2
    F H 2
    M M 2
    C C 1
    D D 1
    D N 1
    E N 1
    G D 1
    G R 1
    H D 1
    I I 1
    L L 1
    M E 1
    M I 1
    M L 1
    V I 1
    V L 1
    V M 1
    V V 1
    V W 1
    W I 1
    W W 1
    Y Y 1
    F D -1
    F M -1
    I D -1
    M A -1
    T T -1
    V D -1
    W D -1
    W F -1
